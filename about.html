About Orgence

Intelligence before implementation.

Orgence was not built from theory or vendor partnerships. It was built from years of operating inside complex business environments — and recognising a structural gap that strategy alone could not fill.

An evolution of Armstrat — a management consulting firm focused on operational strategy, governance design, and execution systems for growing businesses.

Built on Operational Experience

Armstrat has operated across e-commerce, supply chain, and multi-entity business environments.

The Gap
We Observed

"Companies were not struggling with ambition. They were struggling with sequencing."

As AI moved onto the executive agenda, leadership teams began asking the right questions — in the wrong environment.

What leadership was asking
  • — Should we implement AI?
  • — Where can AI reduce cost?
  • — Are we falling behind competitors?
  • — Which tools should we invest in?
What we found inside
  • — Fragmented data environments
  • — Unclear process ownership
  • — Decisions tied to individuals, not systems
  • — Technology layered onto unstable foundations

The issue was not technical capability. It was organizational readiness. AI was being treated as a solution before the organization was structurally prepared for it. Orgence was formed to address that gap.
